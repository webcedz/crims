About Us

CrimProgram is created with the purpose of educating people about Philippine law, its history, and the field of criminology in a clear and accessible way. Developed by criminology students, this platform uses a modern approach to learning by providing information through a website that anyone can easily access online. Our goal is to make criminology topics easier to understand by allowing users to quickly search for subjects and find relevant, reliable content. Through this initiative, we aim to help expand available learning resources and support individuals who are interested in studying criminology or gaining knowledge about the justice system.
