1st PILLAR: THE LAW OF ENFORCEMENT

Law Enforcement Pillar (Law Enforcement)

➤ The prime mover of the system or the initiator of the criminal justice cycle. The front liner in the process.
➤ The first pillar of the Philippine Criminal Justice System consists mainly of the PNP.
➤ The PNP shall take the lead in the investigation and gathering of evidence. They respond to any violation of peace and safety in the community.
➤ Its primary goal is crime prevention.
➤ This pillar also involves different law enforcement agencies under the Executive branch of the govemment such as NBI. PDEA. Bureau of Customs, Bureau of Immigration, and Philippine Coast Guard.
